About Our School

Discover what makes RKP School (Madina) a place of excellence and innovation

Excellence in Education

RKP School (Madina) has been a cornerstone of quality education in our community for many years. We are committed to providing a nurturing environment where students can develop academically, socially, and personally.

Our dedicated faculty, modern facilities, and comprehensive curriculum ensure that every student receives the best possible education to prepare them for future success.

We believe in holistic development, combining academic excellence with character building, sports, and extracurricular activities to create well-rounded individuals.

Admission Process - NO ADMISSION FEES!

FREE ADMISSION

No Admission Fees for Any Grade Level!

At RKP School (Madina), we believe education should be accessible to all deserving students. That's why we have ABSOLUTELY NO ADMISSION FEES for any grade level from Nursery to Class 12.

Merit-Based Scholarships Available

Scholarship Opportunity: Students who perform exceptionally well in our admission test may qualify for scholarships covering tuition fees. Academic excellence is rewarded!

We offer admissions from Nursery to Class 12, with a focus on holistic development and academic excellence. Our experienced faculty and modern facilities provide the perfect environment for your child's educational journey.

Free Application

Complete our online application form with all required documents - NO APPLICATION FEES!

Entrance Test

Take our entrance assessment to evaluate academic readiness and qualify for merit-based scholarships

Interview

Meet with our admission committee for a personal interview

Final Admission

Complete the admission formalities and join our school family - COMPLETELY FREE! Only Fees per Month

Important Information

  • Admission to RKP School (Madina) is completely free for all grade levels
  • Scholarships are awarded based on merit in the entrance test
  • High-performing students may receive full or partial tuition fee waivers
  • All students receive equal opportunities for academic excellence
