Detailed Admission Information

Admission Criteria
  • Age Eligibility: As per CBSE guidelines
  • Documents Required: Birth certificate, previous school records
  • Medical Fitness: Basic health check-up
  • Transfer Certificate: From previous school (if applicable)
Entrance Examination
  • Exam Type: Written assessment (Math, English, General Knowledge)
  • Duration: 60 minutes
  • Format: Multiple choice questions + basic writing
  • Passing Score: Minimum 50% for admission consideration
Merit-Based Cutoffs
  • 90%+ Score: Full scholarship (100% fee waiver)
  • 80-89% Score: 75% scholarship
  • 70-79% Score: 50% scholarship
  • 60-69% Score: 25% scholarship
  • Below 60%: Regular admission (no fees)
Scholarship Details
  • Merit Test: Conducted quarterly for existing students
  • Renewal: Annual performance review required
  • Additional: Sports, arts, and special talent scholarships
  • Financial Aid: Available for deserving cases
Important Dates
Application Deadline: March 31st
Entrance Test: April 1st - 15th
Results Declaration: April 20th
Admission Confirmation: April 21st - 30th
Session Begins: June 1st
Late Admission: Till July 31st (limited seats)

Remember

  • Zero Admission Fees for all grades
  • Merit Scholarships for top performers
  • Equal Opportunity for all students
